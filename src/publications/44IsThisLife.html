Is This Life?
By: Abbas Moosajee

In the darkness of dawn,
The street lamp casts
Your shadow on the curb
The burning world behind
Forces tears out of you.
You shut your eyes-

is this Life?
Always running from
their scarred history,
On the path unknown.
Petrified of being weak,
of losing control.
Forgetting the brevity of life,
The reality of death.
is this Life?

Working to pay, not to Thrive.
Hoping to survive, not to Live.
All this for their blood,
Et tu, Brute?
Yet trying to Love,
is this Life?

So bothered by others’ opinions
So consumed by their anger,
Abstaining from their own desires.
Focusing too long on their
regrets,
Forgetting life is too short for dreams.
is this Life?

I see you kneeled over.
I hear the tremble in your voice.
I recognise the desire for answers.
I remember that burning inside.
But I also remember,
Life wasn’t over.
That is why I urge you to
open your eyes and look past,
Past the world in flames, --To see the
ashen saplings growing. Past the surrounding Brutes,
--To find the gentle one.
Past the inevitability of death,
--For all the choices that remain.

And realise that no matter,
The atrocities of yesteryears,
Or the truths of tomorrow,
Today is in your control.
Beta, this... is Life?
