FRAGILE
By: Ella Kreifeldt

About the Artist

Ella Kreifeldt (15) is an artist interested in digital art. She is passionate about environmental and climate change. She cares deeply about climate change and wants to spread more awareness about this issue. She has made many different environmental related pieces, and currently sell them as stickers on her Etsy. A portion of each profit goes to the Natural Resources Defense Council. She has also made a piece about equality in love, and a few about self love and mindfulness. She plans to find more organizations to donate her profits and sell future posts that are activism related. You can support Ella's work on Etsy and see more of her art on instagram (@jellabeanninc).

About the Artwork

Fragile is a digital art piece that raises awareness on environmental change. It reflects the fragility of the environment and the need to handle it with care.
