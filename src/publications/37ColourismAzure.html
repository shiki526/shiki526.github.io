Artist: Azure

About the artist:

Azure is 16 and enjoys art and too many random things :).

Title of artwork:

Colourism

How does your piece amplify or raise awareness on socio-political issue(s)?

Colourism is a big problem all over the world, especially in places of Asia, where pale skin is glorified. I just want to show that no matter what colour you are, you are still beautiful.
