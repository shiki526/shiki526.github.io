Silence is Violence

By: Huiwen Chen

Silence is Violence, Huiwen Chen 2020

About the Artwork

Anti-Blackness can be a difficult issue to discuss in many Asian communities. This piece, with the words "silence is violence" written in Chinese, urges the Chinese American community to stop turning a blind eye or staying silent in the face of social injustice. As Asians, our complicity is violence against the Black community. We must actively fight against systemic racism. Black Lives Matter.
