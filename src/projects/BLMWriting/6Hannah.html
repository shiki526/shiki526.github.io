Oh the Colours, Oh the Shades

Poet: Hannah Zuta

I look around
And what do I see?
All the colours
Surrounding me.

I was blind
But now I can see,
All the shades
Surrounding me.

They change
The farther you go along.
The more to see
The more to admire

No longer a blip,
But a ray of confidence shining through me.
Oh, how fun it is to finally see
All the shades and colours surrounding me.

How does your work connect to the BLM movement or systemic racism?

It offers people who may not have access to the black perspective a chance to see how some perceive the world. it offers them a chance to see things through our lens.
