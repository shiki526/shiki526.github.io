About
Who We Are
Detester Magazine, founded in 2019, is a space and platform dedicated to inspiring BIPOC youth activism and amplifying socio-political issues. We truly believe that art and writing are powerful mediums for change. We want to amplify all issues that affect BIPOC communities, whether it concerns politics, society, culture, mental health, environment, etc. We aim to push past the endless single stories to deliver a picture that expresses distilled human emotions and diverse perspectives. We are BIPOC youth-led and for BIPOC youth. One of our fundamental pillar is artivism: the intersection between art and activism.
Our Mission
1. Amplify socio-political issues that affect minorities
2. Empower creative BIPOC youth to raise their voices on issues that matter to them
3. Inspire creative BIPOC youth to be socially and politically active
